Calculation Methodology
1. Calculation Errors
Total Variable Budget × Error Rate
Total Variable Budget = (OTE × (1 − Base%)) × Team Size
2. Admin Overhead
Admin Hours/Month × Rate × 12
Covers manual reviews, dispute resolution, shadow accounting
3. Turnover Replacement
Comp-Driven Departures × (Base Salary × Multiplier)
Comp Departures = Team Size × Turnover Rate × Comp-Driven %
4. Ramp Productivity Loss
Departures × Monthly Rev/Rep × Ramp Months × 50%
New hires at ~50% capacity during ramp period
5. Revenue Leakage (Gaming)
Annual Revenue × Gaming Loss %
Sandbagging, deal-deferral, and cherry-picking behaviors
6. Legal & Dispute Risk
Probability of Issue × Average Legal Cost
Probability-weighted annual cost
